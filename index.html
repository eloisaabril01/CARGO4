About Cargo Express Logistics

Founded in 2005, Cargo Express Logistics has grown from a small regional operator to a global logistics provider trusted by businesses of all sizes. Our journey has been defined by our commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

With a team of over 2,500 logistics professionals worldwide, we combine industry expertise with cutting-edge technology to deliver seamless transportation solutions that help our clients succeed in today's competitive global marketplace.
